DECODE: The Game

THE EXPERIENCE — Learn by Doing

A 30-45 minute board game for 4-6 players. Investigate content cards using DECODE method cards, debate with other players, and vote on what's real, misleading, or fake. Every card has a QR code linking to related comics and videos.

Phase 1

THE FEED

5 min

Draw 5 Content Cards — news, posts, videos, memes. Classify each as REAL, MISLEADING, or FAKE.

Phase 2

THE INVESTIGATION

15 min

Use DECODE Cards to fact-check. Handle Event Cards (AI Deepfake Alert! Bot Network Activated!). Debate and persuade.

Phase 3

THE VERDICT

10 min

Community vote → Compare with Truth Cards → Score points → Reflection: "Why were we fooled?"

The Method

The DECODE Framework

Six steps to decode any piece of information. The name IS the method. Easy to remember, powerful to apply.

D

Detect

"Is something off? Am I feeling emotionally triggered?"

Recognize red flags: sensational headlines, urgent language, emotional manipulation, too-good-to-be-true claims.

E

Evidence

"What's the source? Is there concrete proof?"

Look for citations, data, named experts, verifiable facts. No evidence = no credibility.

C

Cross-check

"What do other sources say? Can anyone confirm?"

Check at least 3 independent sources. If only one source reports it, be skeptical.

O

Origin

"Who created this? Real person, bot, or AI?"

Investigate the creator's identity, history, and credibility. Check for bot patterns, AI watermarks, account age.

D

Determine Motive

"Why was this created? Who benefits?"

Follow the money, power, or attention. Is it clickbait? Propaganda? Satire? Understanding motive reveals intent.

E

Evaluate Impact

"If I share this, what happens? Who gets hurt?"

Consider consequences before sharing. Your share button is a megaphone — use it responsibly.
