# DECODE: The Game — Complete Rules

## 🎲 Overview
**DECODE: The Game** is a cooperative-competitive board game where 4-6 players investigate social media content to determine what's REAL, MISLEADING, or FAKE — using the DECODE method.

**Players:** 4-6 | **Time:** 30-45 minutes | **Age:** 15+ | **Language:** English (localizable)

---

## 📦 Components (Print-and-Play)

| Component | Quantity | Description |
|-----------|----------|-------------|
| Content Cards | 60 | News headlines, social media posts, memes, videos — some real, some fake |
| DECODE Cards | 30 | Fact-checking tools, 5 of each type (D, E, C, O, D, E) |
| Event Cards | 15 | Random events that change the game dynamics |
| Truth Cards | 60 | Answer cards — reveal if Content Card is REAL/MISLEADING/FAKE |
| Score Tokens | 30 | Track player points |
| Player Role Cards | 6 | Secret roles (Investigator × 4, Disinformator × 2) |
| DECODE Reference Card | 6 | Quick reference for the 6-step method |
| QR Code Directory | 1 | List of QR codes linking to Comics & Videos |

---

## 🎯 Objective

**Investigators** want to correctly identify all Content Cards.
**Disinformators** (secret role) want to mislead other players into wrong classifications.

The player with the highest score at the end wins — but the real winner is everyone who learns to think critically!

---

## 📋 Setup (2 minutes)

1. **Shuffle** all Content Cards face-down in the center
2. **Deal** 1 Role Card face-down to each player (keep secret!)
3. **Place** DECODE Cards in 6 piles by type
4. **Shuffle** Event Cards into a separate deck
5. **Give** each player 1 DECODE Reference Card and 5 Score Tokens
6. **Choose** a Game Master (can also play) to hold Truth Cards

---

## 🔄 Game Flow — 3 Phases

### PHASE 1: "THE FEED" (5 minutes)

1. Flip **5 Content Cards** face-up in the center
2. Each Content Card shows:
   - A headline, social media post, or image
   - Source information (or lack thereof)
   - A QR code (links to related DECODE Chronicles webtoon chapter)
   - A number (matches a Truth Card)
3. Each player silently reads all 5 cards
4. Each player places a **preliminary vote token** on each card:
   - 🟢 REAL | 🟡 MISLEADING | 🔴 FAKE

### PHASE 2: "THE INVESTIGATION" (15-20 minutes)

This is where DECODE happens! Players take turns clockwise.

**On your turn:**
1. **Draw 1 DECODE Card** from any pile:
   - 🔍 **D - Detect**: Reveal one red flag hint on any Content Card
   - 📊 **E - Evidence**: Ask the Game Master for one factual detail
   - 🌐 **C - Cross-check**: Compare two Content Cards for consistency
   - 🧠 **O - Origin**: Learn about the creator/source of a Content Card
   - 💰 **D - Determine Motive**: Reveal the financial/political motivation
   - ⚖️ **E - Evaluate Impact**: Learn who would be affected if this spreads

2. **Play the DECODE Card** on a Content Card of your choice
3. **The Game Master reads** the corresponding info from the Truth Card
4. **Open Discussion** (30 seconds): Any player can argue to change votes

**EVENT CARDS**: After every 2 rounds, draw 1 Event Card:
- 🤖 "AI DEEPFAKE ALERT!" — One Content Card is replaced with an AI-generated version
- 🌐 "BOT NETWORK ACTIVATED" — A false "cross-check" confirmation appears
- ⏰ "BREAKING NEWS!" — A new Content Card is added under time pressure (15 seconds to vote)
- 🔒 "SOURCE BLOCKED" — One DECODE card type is unavailable this round
- 📢 "VIRAL PRESSURE" — All players must defend their votes out loud

**DISINFORMATOR SPECIAL**: Secret Disinformators can:
- Play misleading DECODE cards (give wrong interpretation)
- Argue convincingly for wrong classifications
- They earn points for each player they mislead

### PHASE 3: "THE VERDICT" (10 minutes)

1. **Final Vote**: All players place their final vote on each Content Card
2. **Reveal**: Game Master flips corresponding Truth Cards
3. **Scoring**:
   - ✅ Correct classification: **+3 points**
   - ❌ Wrong classification: **-1 point**
   - 🗣️ Successfully convinced another player (who changed to correct): **+2 points**
   - 🕵️ Identified a Disinformator: **+5 points**
   - 🎭 Disinformator: **+2 points per player misled**

4. **Reflection Round** (most important!):
   - "Which card fooled you and why?"
   - "What DECODE step would have caught it?"
   - "Have you seen something like this in real life?"

## 📊 Content Card Categories

Each Content Card belongs to one of 6 themes (matching Video episodes & Comics chapters):

| Theme | Color | Examples |
|-------|-------|---------|
| Fake News & Viral Content | 🔴 Red | Fabricated health scares, political hoaxes |
| Bot Networks & Fake Accounts | 🟠 Orange | Coordinated campaigns, fake reviews |
| AI-Generated Content | 🟣 Purple | Deepfakes, AI articles, synthetic images |
| Filter Bubbles & Algorithms | 🔵 Blue | Recommendation manipulation, echo chambers |
| Data Privacy & Exploitation | 🟢 Green | Phishing, data harvesting, targeted ads |
| Responsible Content Creation | 🟡 Yellow | Satire vs. misinformation, ethical sharing |

---

## 🏆 Winning

**Individual Winner**: Player with highest score
**Team Achievement**: If the group correctly identifies 4+ out of 5 Content Cards

**The REAL victory**: Can you apply DECODE in your daily social media use?

---

## 📱 QR Code Integration (Transmedia Connection)

Each Content Card has a QR code on the back:
- **Scan after playing** to read the related DECODE Chronicles webtoon chapter
- The chapter tells the backstory of the scenario in the Content Card
- Characters from the webtoon face the same disinformation challenge
- After reading, watch the corresponding DECODE Stories video episode

**Completion Tracking**: Complete all 6 scenarios to unlock the full DECODE Universe!

## 🖨️ Print-and-Play Instructions

1. Download the PDF from [decode-universe.org/game]
2. Print on A4/Letter paper (color recommended, B&W works too)
3. Cut along the dotted lines
4. Optional: Laminate for durability
5. You're ready to play!

**Cost: $0** — Only a printer and scissors needed.

## 🌍 Localization Guide

To adapt DECODE for your country:
1. Replace Content Card scenarios with local examples
2. Translate game rules (this document)
3. Keep the DECODE framework universal
4. Create new Truth Cards for localized content
5. Submit your version to the DECODE community for sharing
