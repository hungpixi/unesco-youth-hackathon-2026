## 1. PROBLEM STATEMENT

### The Challenge
In 2026, the global information landscape faces an unprecedented crisis. Vietnam — with 79 million social media users — exemplifies a worldwide pattern: young people are drowning in content but lack the critical thinking tools to navigate it.

**Key statistics:**
- **72%** of Vietnamese youth share content without source verification
- **68%** of Gen Z cannot distinguish AI-generated images from real photos
- **5.2 hours** average daily screen time for Vietnamese youth
- **117,000+** pieces of harmful content removed in H1 2026 in Vietnam
- **50 million VND** maximum fine under Vietnam's Decree 174/2026 for spreading fake news

### Why This Matters Now
The convergence of three trends makes 2026 a critical inflection point:
1. **AI-powered disinformation** (deepfakes, synthetic media) is now accessible to anyone
2. **Algorithm-driven echo chambers** amplify emotional content 6x faster than factual content
3. **Youth digital dependency** makes them the most exposed and least equipped demographic

**The gap:** Governments respond with regulation (fines, content removal), but punishment treats symptoms, not causes. What young people need is **education** — delivered in formats they actually engage with.

## 2. PROPOSED SOLUTION: DECODE UNIVERSE

### Overview
DECODE is a **transmedia education ecosystem** that teaches Media and Information Literacy (MIL) through three interconnected mediums:

| Pillar | Name | Role | Format |
|--------|------|------|--------|
| 📺 Video | DECODE Stories | **HOOK** — Sparks curiosity | 6-episode mini-documentary series (3-5 min each) |
| 🎲 Game | DECODE: The Game | **EXPERIENCE** — Learn by doing | Board game, 4-6 players, 30-45 minutes |
| 📖 Comics | DECODE Chronicles | **DEEP DIVE** — Empathy through story | 6-chapter webtoon (manga/manhwa style) |

### What Makes DECODE Different

**Transmedia storytelling:** Unlike previous hackathon entries that created standalone tools, DECODE builds a unified story universe where each medium tells a different part of the same narrative. Characters from the comics appear in the videos. Scenarios from the videos become Content Cards in the board game. Game cards link via QR codes to comic chapters.

**This is the first transmedia MIL education ecosystem ever proposed.**

### The DECODE Framework
At the core of everything is a 6-step critical thinking method — where the name IS the method:

| Step | Action | Guiding Question |
|------|--------|-----------------|
| **D** — Detect | Identify red flags | "Is something unusual? Am I being emotionally manipulated?" |
| **E** — Evidence | Check for proof | "What's the source? Is there verifiable evidence?" |
| **C** — Cross-check | Verify with multiple sources | "Do other credible sources confirm this?" |
| **O** — Origin | Investigate the creator | "Who made this? Real person, bot, or AI?" |
| **D** — Determine motive | Understand intent | "Why was this created? Who benefits?" |
| **E** — Evaluate impact | Consider consequences | "If I share this and it's wrong, who gets hurt?" |

## 4. DETAILED SOLUTION DESIGN

### 4.1 DECODE Stories (Video Series)

**6 episodes**, each tackling a different MIL competency:
1. "The Viral Trap" — ACCESS (how we choose what to consume)
2. "Who's Behind the Screen?" — EVALUATE (identifying fake accounts/bots)
3. "AI Wrote This" — UNDERSTAND (AI-generated content detection)
4. "The Algorithm Knows You" — ANALYZE (filter bubbles, echo chambers)
5. "Your Data, Their Profit" — EVALUATE (privacy, data exploitation)
6. "Be the Signal, Not the Noise" — CREATE (responsible content creation)

**Production approach:** Smartphone + AI editing tools (CapCut, Canva) = low cost, high quality.

### 4.2 DECODE: The Game (Board Game)

**Format:** Cooperative-competitive, 4-6 players, 30-45 minutes

**Three phases:**
- **THE FEED** (5 min): Draw Content Cards showing news/posts/memes
- **THE INVESTIGATION** (15 min): Use DECODE Cards to fact-check, handle Event Cards
- **THE VERDICT** (10 min): Vote, reveal truth, score, reflect

**Key innovation:** Every Content Card has a QR code linking to the related webtoon chapter and video episode.

**Distribution:** **Print-and-Play PDF** — free download, any school can print and play immediately. Zero budget required.

### 4.3 DECODE Chronicles (Webtoon Comics)

**6 chapters**, each following a different character from a different country:
- Minh (19, 🇻🇳 Vietnam) — Accidental spreader → critical thinker
- Aisha (22, 🇳🇬 Nigeria) — Journalism student vs. bot farm
- Kai (20, 🇯🇵 Japan) — AI enthusiast vs. deepfakes
- Luna (21, 🇧🇷 Brazil) — Content creator vs. data theft
- Sam (18, 🇺🇸 USA) — High schooler vs. cyberbullying
- Priya (23, 🇮🇳 India) — Coder builds fact-checking tool

**Format:** Vertical scroll (mobile-first), manga/manhwa art style, bilingual (Vietnamese + English).

## 5. INNOVATION

DECODE's innovation lies in three areas:

1. **Transmedia integration**: No previous MIL project has created a cohesive narrative universe spanning video, games, and comics with cross-references between all three.

2. **Framework = Brand = Action**: The DECODE method is simultaneously the project name, the educational framework, and a verb ("Let me DECODE this"). This creates instant brand recognition and behavioral prompts.

3. **AI Literacy native**: Unlike older MIL frameworks (3C2B, CRAAP test), DECODE was designed from the ground up to address AI-generated content, deepfakes, and synthetic media — the defining challenge of 2026.
