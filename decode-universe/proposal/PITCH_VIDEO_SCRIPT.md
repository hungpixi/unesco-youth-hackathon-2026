## 1:10 - 1:40 | THE METHOD

**[VISUAL]**: DECODE framework — letters appear with color-coded animations

**[VO]**:
"At the core is the DECODE method. Six steps that anyone can learn:"

**[Each letter animates in sequence]**:
- **D** — "Detect red flags. Is the headline trying to manipulate your emotions?"
- **E** — "Look for Evidence. Are there sources? Data? Named experts?"
- **C** — "Cross-check. Do other sources confirm this?"
- **O** — "Origin. Who created this? A real person, a bot, or AI?"
- **D** — "Determine motive. Who benefits from you believing this?"
- **E** — "Evaluate impact. If you share this and it's wrong — who gets hurt?"

**[VO]**:
"The name IS the method. DECODE. Six letters. Sixty seconds. One informed decision."
