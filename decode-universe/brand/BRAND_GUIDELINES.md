# DECODE — Brand Guidelines

## Brand Identity

### Name & Tagline
- **Full name**: DECODE — See Through the Noise
- **Short name**: DECODE
- **Tagline**: See Through the Noise
- **Framework name**: The DECODE Method

### Logo Concept
- **Icon**: 🔓 (Unlock/Decode symbol)
- **Typography**: "DECODE" in Space Grotesk Bold, letter-spacing: 2px
- **Usage**: Icon + text side by side, or icon only for small sizes

### Design Principles
1. **Sáng màu, không tối** — Light backgrounds, dark text
2. **Hiện đại, không cổ điển** — Clean lines, generous whitespace
3. **Thân thiện, không đe dọa** — Approachable feel, not scary
4. **Giáo dục qua trải nghiệm** — Show don't tell
5. **Nhất quán xuyên suốt** — Same palette/typography across video, game, comics, web

### Voice & Tone
- **Conversational**, not academic
- **Empowering**, not preachy
- **Direct**, not complicated
- **Inclusive**, not exclusive
- **Curious**, not judgmental
- Example: "Let's DECODE this together" ✅ vs "You must learn to be more critical" ❌
