# DECODE Chronicles — Chapter 1: "The Viral Trap"

## PANEL SEQUENCE

### PAGE 1 — Opening (4 panels)

**Panel 1** (Full width, establishing shot)
- **Visual**: Bird's eye view of a Vietnamese university campus at golden hour. Students walking, motorbikes parked. Modern buildings with trees.
- **Caption**: "Hanoi, Vietnam. 7:42 PM."

**Panel 2** (Medium shot)
- **Visual**: Minh sitting at a coffee shop near campus, laptop open, phone in hand. He's scrolling TikTok. Around him, friends are chatting. He's in his own world.
- **Caption**: "Minh, 19. Computer Science major. 4.7 hours of screen time per day."
- **Minh's thought bubble**: *"Just 5 more minutes..."*

**Panel 3** (Close-up on phone screen)
- **Visual**: Phone showing a social media feed. Multiple posts scrolling. One post highlighted with a red alert emoji:
  > 🚨 BỘ Y TẾ CẢNH BÁO: Virus mới lây qua nước máy. Phải đun sôi ngay! Chia sẻ để cứu người! 🚨
- **Caption**: "Then he sees it."

**Panel 4** (Minh's face — concerned expression)
- **Visual**: Close-up of Minh's face, eyebrows raised, eyes widening.
- **Minh**: "Ê, cái này ghê quá..."
- **English subtitle**: *(Whoa, this is serious...)*

### PAGE 2 — The Share (5 panels)

**Panel 5** (Minh looking at post)
- **Visual**: Minh squinting at the post. Small text shows "2,345 shares • 892 comments"
- **Minh's thought**: *"2,000 shares already... this must be legit."*

**Panel 6** (Thumb hovering over SHARE button)
- **Visual**: Extreme close-up of Minh's thumb over the Share button. The button glows slightly.
- **SFX text**: *tap*
- **Caption**: "It takes 0.8 seconds to share. It takes 0 seconds to not think."

**Panel 7** (Share confirmed — ripple effect)
- **Visual**: The post appears on Minh's profile. Ripple circles emanating outward. Notification count: "Shared to 847 friends"
- **Minh**: "Mom's group needs to see this too."
- *He forwards it to a family Zalo group*

**Panel 8** (Time-lapse montage)
- **Visual**: Split panel showing 4 moments:
  - Minh's mom forwarding to HER group (30 people)
  - A neighbor reading it, looking worried
  - A local news Facebook page reposting it
  - A counter climbing: 5K → 15K → 50K shares
- **Caption**: "In 3 hours, Minh's share reaches 50,000 people."

**Panel 9** (Chaos panel)
- **Visual**: Montage of consequences:
  - Supermarket shelves being emptied (bottled water)
  - People panicking on social media comments
  - Hospital phone lines overwhelmed
- **Caption**: "Panic spreads faster than any virus."

---

### PAGE 3 — The Fallout (5 panels)

**Panel 10** (Next morning — university)
- **Visual**: Minh walking into class. Phone buzzing with notifications. Classmates looking at him.
- **Caption**: "The next morning."

**Panel 11** (Professor speaking)
- **Visual**: Professor standing in front of class, serious expression. Projector shows: "MINISTRY OF HEALTH: NO VIRUS IN TAP WATER — FAKE NEWS"
- **Professor**: "One of our students helped spread a hoax yesterday. The Ministry had to issue an official denial."

**Panel 12** (Minh sinking in chair)
- **Visual**: Minh slowly sinking in his chair. Sweat drops. Other students glancing at him.
- **Minh's thought**: *"That was me... oh no..."*

**Panel 13** (Phone screen — official statement)
- **Visual**: Minh's phone showing the Ministry of Health's official denial. Next to it, comments on his post: "Fake news bro", "Check before you share 🤦", "Reported"
- **Caption**: "The original post was from an account created 3 days ago. 12 followers. No profile photo."

**Panel 14** (Minh staring at phone in empty hallway)
- **Visual**: After class. Minh alone in the hallway, looking at his phone. The original post has been deleted. But screenshots of his share are everywhere.
- **Minh**: "I can delete my post... but I can't delete what already happened."

### PAGE 4 — The Awakening (6 panels)

**Panel 15** (Coffee shop — different day)
- **Visual**: Same coffee shop as Panel 2, but now Minh is alone. No laptop. Just sitting, thinking.
- **Caption**: "3 days later."

**Panel 16** (Flashback panels)
- **Visual**: 3 small panels showing Minh's memory:
  1. His mom calling him, upset: "Mấy người hàng xóm mua sạch nước rồi con ơi!"
  2. A news article: "Fake water virus hoax causes panic buying in Hanoi"
  3. His university's announcement about responsible social media use
- **Minh's thought**: *"All because I pressed one button."*

**Panel 17** (Friend sits down — Linh appears)
- **Visual**: A girl (Linh, 20, media studies student) sits across from Minh. She puts her laptop down, showing a presentation: "DECODE: See Through the Noise"
- **Linh**: "Minh. Tao thấy bài đăng của mày. Mày có muốn học cách để chuyện đó không xảy ra lần nữa không?"
- **English subtitle**: *("Minh. I saw your post. Want to learn how to never let that happen again?")*

**Panel 18** (Minh looks up — spark of interest)
- **Visual**: Minh looks up at Linh. For the first time, his expression changes from guilt to curiosity.
- **Minh**: "DECODE?"

**Panel 19** (Linh explains — DECODE visual)
- **Visual**: Linh draws on a napkin. The 6 letters D-E-C-O-D-E with icons next to each.
- **Linh**: "6 bước. Trước khi share bất cứ thứ gì. Bắt đầu bằng D — Detect. Có gì bất thường không?"
- **English**: *(6 steps. Before you share anything. Start with D — Detect. Is anything unusual?)*

**Panel 20** (Split panel — Before vs After)
- **Visual**: Left: Minh's old self, scrolling mindlessly, tapping Share.
  Right: The new question in his mind — "Wait. Let me DECODE this first."
- **Caption**: "It was the end of the old Minh. And the beginning of something new."

### PAGE 5 — The First DECODE (6 panels)

**Panel 21** (A week later — Minh sees another viral post)
- **Visual**: Minh scrolling his feed. Another urgent post appears:
  > "Trường ĐH Bách Khoa bị hack! Tất cả điểm thi bị xóa! Chia sẻ ngay!"
- **Caption**: "One week later. Another viral post. But this time..."

**Panel 22** (D — Detect)
- **Visual**: Minh's eyes narrow. A magnifying glass icon appears. Text highlights the red flags.
- **Minh's thought**: *"D — Detect. Headline dùng toàn dấu chấm than. 'Chia sẻ ngay' = emotional urgency."*
- **Highlighted**: "!!!" and "Chia sẻ ngay!"

**Panel 23** (E — Evidence)
- **Visual**: Minh opens a browser, searching for the claim.
- **Minh's thought**: *"E — Evidence. Tìm trên Google... Website trường không đề cập gì. Không có nguồn."*
- **Search results**: No relevant results found.

**Panel 24** (C — Cross-check)
- **Visual**: Minh checks 3 different news sites. None are reporting this.
- **Minh's thought**: *"C — Cross-check. VnExpress, Tuổi Trẻ, Thanh Niên... không ai đưa tin."*

**Panel 25** (O — Origin)
- **Visual**: Minh clicks on the poster's profile. Account created yesterday. 7 followers. Random profile photo.
- **Minh's thought**: *"O — Origin. Tài khoản mới tạo hôm qua. 7 followers. Ảnh đại diện stock."*

**Panel 26** (Decision moment)
- **Visual**: Minh's thumb hovers over the Share button again — just like Panel 6. But this time, he pulls his thumb AWAY. Instead, he hits REPORT.
- **Minh**: "Không share. Report."
- **Caption**: "The same 0.8 seconds. A completely different choice."
- **SFX**: *REPORT ✓*

## 🎨 ART DIRECTION NOTES

**Style**: Modern manga/manhwa — clean lines, expressive faces, vibrant colors
**Color Palette**: 
- Normal scenes: Warm, bright colors (Hanoi golden hour aesthetic)
- Disinformation moments: Slight red/orange tint, visual noise/static effects
- DECODE moments: Blue-purple gradient highlights, clean and clear
**Panels**: Vertical scroll optimized, varied sizes for pacing
**Text**: Bilingual (Vietnamese primary, English subtitles in brackets)
**Special Effects**: 
- Ripple effects for sharing/spreading
- Magnifying glass overlays for DECODE analysis
- Static/glitch effects for fake content
